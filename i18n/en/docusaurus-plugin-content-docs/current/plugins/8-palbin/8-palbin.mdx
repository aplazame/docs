# Palbin integration
Here you will find everything you need to integrate Aplazame into your Palbin store.

:::tip
  Keep close the **API keys** related to your online store, which you can obtain from your [control panel](https://vendors.aplazame.com/) in the **Preferencias** section.
:::

## Configure the Aplazame payment method
- Access the administration panel of your Palbin store.
- Go to **Configuración > Método de pago > Opciones de pago aplazado** and select the **Aplazame** option in the dropdown if it appears available in your country.
- Configure the payment method with your **API keys**.
- If you want to do a test, activate **Marca esta opción SOLO para realizar pruebas...** and make a test purchase using our [test data](../../api/test-environment/test-data/).
:::warning
  Remember to deactivate **Marca esta opción SOLO para realizar pruebas...** when you finish the tests to leave the payment method in production mode and real orders can be carried out.
:::
